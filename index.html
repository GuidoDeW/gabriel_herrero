Specializations

An explorer by nature, I am skilled at planning and guiding hiking tours, especially those centred around photography.

My greatest passion and expertise lie in photography. Besides having completed close to one hundred projects, I have amassed a vast collection of stock photos of natural landscapes and urban settings.

A picture can be worth more than a thousand words, but many words are still worth writing. I not only chronicle my expeditions on my personal blog, but also offer custom texts on hiking, photography, and related topics.

Inspiration

The flow of nature

Nature is always in motion, yet in the right moment, tranquility can be found in its apparent chaos. In my photography as well as on my hiking tours, I seek to capture and share these moments.

The bigger picture

Many think of photography as a means of focusing on particular details while excluding the rest. Yet it is often by taking the broadest possible view, both literally and figuratively, that we learn to truly appreciate what is right in front of us. This is also what I strive to do on my blog.
