About Me

My Story

A Photographer's Development

Born in the mountainous north of Spain, I spent my childhood blessed with incredible surroundings. From a very young age, I developed an interest in recording these wonderful natural sights. I began researching everything I could about the art and myriad technical aspects of photography, and got my first photography job at 16. I have since developed a tried-and-tested work method, which you can read more about below .

Life in Pictures

After working various jobs for a few years, I committed myself to hiking and photography full-time, both in my home country and abroad. As you can see in my gallery, some of my favourite destinations so far include Scandinavia and Canada. Needless to say, I not only work to travel, but am also quite prepared to travel for work, so wherever you are, feel free to send me a message!
